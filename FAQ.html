FAQs

Account and Login

Q: How do I create an account?
A: To create an account, Go to the Home page and click on the Login/Signup

Q: I forgot my password. How can I reset it?
A: To reset your password, Click on the forgot password option below the Login/Signup

General Information

Q: What technologies are allowed for projects?
A: Participants are free to use any programming languages, frameworks, and technologies for their projects.

Q: Are there any specific themes for the hackathon?
A: Each hackathon may have specific themes or challenges. Please check the event details for more information.

Q: How can I stay updated on hackathon announcements?
A: We regularly update our website and send email notifications. Make sure to follow us on social media for the latest announcements.

Event Logistics

Q: What is the schedule for the hackathon?
A: The schedule, including opening and closing ceremonies, coding periods, and judging sessions, will be available on our website closer to the event date.

Q: Is the hackathon held in person or online?
A: The format of the hackathon (in-person, online, or hybrid) will be specified in the event details. Please check the website for the latest information.

Submission and Judging

Q: How do I submit my project for the hackathon?
A: Submission details will be provided during the event. Typically, you will submit your project through our online platform.

Q: How are projects evaluated?
A: Projects are evaluated based on criteria such as creativity, technical complexity, and impact. Judges may also consider how well teams address the specified themes or challenges.
